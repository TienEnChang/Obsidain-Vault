
$X\sim \text{hyper-geo}(n,\,N,\,R)$
- where
	- param:
		- $n,\,N,\,R\in\{1,\,2,\cdots\,\}$  with  $n,\,R\leq N$
		- $\dfrac{R}{N}=P_{0}(E_{0})$,  $E_{0}=$ draw a red ball 
	- sample space:  $\Omega=\Omega_{0}{^{\!n}}$
	- count variable:
		- $X(\omega)\coloneqq$ $\#$ of $E_{0}$ in $n$ draws
- note
	- ${a \choose b}=0$  if  $b<0$  or  $b>a$
