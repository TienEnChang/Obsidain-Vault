- Petty cash fund
	- person in charge
		- custodian 保管人
	- calc
		- established = remain + receipt (+ cash short - cash over)
			- established = NT$ 100
			- remain = NT$ 15
			- receipt = NT$ 80
			- $\implies$ cash short = NT$ 5
			- $\implies$ replenished = NT$ 85
	- entry
		- | Establish          |  |
		  | ------------------- | -------------------------- |
		  | A: Petty Cash↑ |       |
		  | A: Cash↓   |                 |
		- | Replenish          ||
		  |--|--|
		  |(消耗)|(補回，因為 Petty Cash 會抵銷，所以兩者合併 to 簡化)|
		  | E: Expense↑ | A: Petty Cash↑      |
		  | A: Petty Cash↓  | A: Cash↓                   |
