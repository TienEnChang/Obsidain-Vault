case $b=a^{p-1}$: $ab=A+B$
case $b<a^{p-1}$: $ab=A+B-\displaystyle\int_{b\,^{q-1}}^a(x^{p-1}-b)dx=A+B-[\dfrac{a^p-b^{\frac{p}{q-1}}}{p}-ab+{b\,^{q}}]$
case $b>a^{p-1}$: $ab=A+B-\displaystyle\int_a^{b\,^{q-1}}(b-x^{p-1})dx=A+B-[\dfrac{a^p-b^{\frac{p}{q-1}}}{p}-ab+{b\,^{q}}]$
