					 { i.e. $([T]_{\beta})_{ii}$ is "eigenvalue of $T$" }
	
- $A$ is diagonalizable:
	- $\exists$ invertible $Q \in M_{n}(F)$  s.t. $Q^{-1} A Q$  is diagonal

	- $\text{det}(x I_{n}-[T]_{\beta})\in P_{n}(F)$  for any  $\beta\,$ of $V$
