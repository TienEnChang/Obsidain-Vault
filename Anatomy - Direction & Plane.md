
Brain Directions
(1)
- ↑, ↓: superior(上), inferior(下) = dorsal(背), ventral(腹) axis
- ←, →: anterior(前), posterior(後) = rostral(鼻), caudal(尾) axis
(2)
- medial = at the midline
- lateral = at the sides
